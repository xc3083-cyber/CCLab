[Gravital: Cyber Virus Hunter]

Scientific Name:

A cybernetic organism engineered for viral detection and neutralization.

Discovered:

It was discovered by NYUSH IT while upgrading a router at NYU Shanghai.

Family:

Kingdom: Cybernetica; Phylum: Technobiota; Class: Augmentata; Order: Integrata; Family: Synthorganidae; Genus: Cyberorganismus; Species: Cybernetic organism.

Habitat:

Cyberspace

Appearance:

A pixel-like creature with one eye and five tentacles. It floats in cyberspace and its tentacles move like soft waves while scanning the environment.

Key Characteristics:

  • Constantly patrols and restarts cyberspace
  • love eating tokens and may explode when fed and touched
  • detects red cyber viruses
  • destroys viruses with green laser beams

What we know so far about [ Gravital ]? Gravital was discovered by NYUSH IT while upgrading a router at NYU Shanghai. During the update, this strange cyber creature suddenly appeared inside the network. People have observed that Gravital constantly scans cyberspace.When it detects a red virus, it quickly fires a green laser beam to destroy it. After few seconds,Gravital will try to restart the entire cyberspace.Scientists also found that Gravital loves eating tokens. If you feed it tokens or touch its head, it becomes very happy and explodes into pieces, but it will reassemble itself in a few seconds and continue patrolling.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature 1.Click space to tickle it. 2.Help it with detecting viruses. 3.Feed it with tokens so it will be happy.
